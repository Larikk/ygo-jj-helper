Ruling Questions

The following situations require clarification. The final decisions will be documented on the Ruleset page.

Spell/Trap Limit

Can I activate another Spell/Trap if the first one was negated?
Example: Any Spell, Solumn Judgement and another Spell Possible rulings:
  • Yes
  • No

Quick-Play Spells

Do cards that interact with Normal Spells interact with Quick-Play Spells too?
Example: Dark Balter the Terrible
Possible rulings:
  • Quick-Play Spells are Spell Speed 1 but not Normal Spells (similar to Ritual Spells)
  • Quick-Play Spells are treated as Normal Spells
How do cards that list "Quick-Play spells" work?
Example: Invader of Darkness
Note: This ruling is influenced by the decision of the previous one.
Possible Rulings:
  • They don't
  • They work just like normal
  • They work on normal spells now

Effects related with Tribute Summoning

Effect allows to optionally tribute more than necessary
Example: Moisture Creature
Possible rulings:
  • Tributing is not allowed therefore it is not possible to meet the activation requirement
  • "Tribute X" turns into "Tribute 0", effect triggers always
  • Monster can be Normal Summoned without activating the effect but also be Tribute Summoned with the specified amount of tributes to trigger their effect (some members in #junior-format use this ruling)
Effect activates when a monster is Tribute Summoned

Example: Monarchs
Possible rulings:

  • Monsters are Normal Summoned, not Tribute Summoned, therefore their effects can not be activated
  • Effect is activated on Normal Summon
  • Monster can either be Normal Summoned without effect or Tribute Summoned with effect

Effect text requires a specific amount or type of Tributes
Examples: Obelisk the Tormentor, Victory Dragon
Possible rulings:
  • Monster can not be summoned at all because tributing for Normal/Tribute Summon is not possible
  • Monster can be Normal Summoned without any Tributes
  • Monster must be Tribute Summoned with Tributes
