Deck Validation [Experimental]

Takes the contents of a YDK file and checks whether the included cards were available in the selected year. Not all YDK files are supported; Some applications/web sites use unofficial passcodes for alternative artworks (like EDOpro) which this tool does not support. YDKs from Duelingbook are fine though.
